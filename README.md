# Description

# Data files
- combined_bear_model_data.csv : weekly independent detections of bear species and predictor variables for the habitat use models
- stations.csv: geographical data for camera trap stations (AVAILABLE AT REQUEST)
- BLT_detection_data_urs_hom.csv : raw detections of bears and humans by camera trap station
- BLT_30min_Independent.csv : independent detections of species by camera station in Mount Robson Park 
- Full_BLT_Trail.gpkg : GIS file of trail features

## DATA-SPECIFIC INFORMATION FOR: combined_bear_model_data.csv
Deployment.Location.ID: camera trap station ID
Species: latin name for bear species detected
Species_Label: common name for bear species detected
Week_Label: unique week identifier in ISO week notation (i.e., YYYY_Www)
Week_Start: First day of the study week
Bear_Detections: Number of individuals of a given bear species detected in a given survey week
Effort: weekly effort at Deployment.Location.ID
log_effort:log transformed effort used as an effort offset term
mean_ndvi: Mean Normalized Difference in Vegetation Index per week at each CT site, measured in 16-day intervals (250-meter resolution)
mean_ndvi_scaled: mean_ndvi scaled by subtracting the mean and dividing by SD
elevation: elevation in meters above sea level.
elevation_scaled: elevation scaled by subtracting the mean and dividing by SD
canopy: percentage of vegetation taller than 2m (100m radius around CT site)
canopy_scaled: canopy_cover scaled by subtracting the mean and dividing by SD
dist_trail: distance from nearest trail to the camera trap station 
prox_trail: proximity to the nearest trail, calculated as an exponential decay of distance (exp(-dist_trail)/ 500 meters)
prox_trail_scaled: prox_trail scaled by subtracting the mean and dividing by SD
rec_week:  rate of human detections per 100 camera trap days for a given week and camera trap site
rec_week_scaled: standardized log-transformed weekly human detections, log transformed to reduce skew, then scaled (mean= 0, SD = 1)
season: defines whether a bear detection was before (September-November) or after (May-August) the annual torpor period.
trail_status: whether the section of trail that this camera trap station was located was open or closed within a given week (NA for all off-trail sites)

## DATA-SPECIFIC INFORMATION FOR: stations.csv
AVAILABLE AT REQUEST
station_id: camera trap station ID (also "Deployment.Location.ID")
project_id: BLT = Berg Lake Trail Camera Trap Project
latitude: latitude of Deployment.Location.ID
longitude: longitude of Deployment.Location.ID
site_location: notes on specific camera trap location including reopening phase, area description, habitat type, elevation, camera height, and bearing
site_comments: deployment date and method.


## DATA-SPECIFIC INFORMATION FOR: BLT_detection_data_urs_hom.csv
Project.ID: BLT = Berg Lake Trail Camera Trap Project
Image.ID: image file name, structure as "Deployment.Location.ID__YYYY_MM_DD__HH_MM_SS.jpg"
Blank: TRUE = no animal detected by MegaDetector, FALSE = animal detected by Megadetector
Species: species detected (latin/scientific name)
Species.Common.Name: common name for species detected
Date_Time.Captured: date and time of image capture, structures "YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM"
Time.Zone: time zone of camera trap site (UTC-8)
Number.of.Animals: count of individuals detected in a given image
Minimum.Group.Size: total group size of species detected in a single event (i.e., number of animals of a given species and site within a 30 minute period)
